FAQ

Are events free?

Most are free (visits, wellbeing, equality forums). Some special celebrations are ticketed to cover costs.

Can I bring my family?

Yes! Many events are family-friendly. We’ll specify any age guidance on each event.

How soon will you reply?

We aim to respond within 2–3 business days.

Privacy

We only use your details to respond to your enquiry and share WIN updates you’ve asked for. You can opt out anytime.
