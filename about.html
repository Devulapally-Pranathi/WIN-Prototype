About WIN

People first. Faithful to community.

We’re a volunteer-led network uniting communities across Wyndham through dialogue, celebration, wellbeing, equality and caring for the environment.

Join the Network Our Mission

Our Mission

Create welcoming spaces where people of all faiths (and none) connect, learn and work together for the common good.

Our Vision

A Wyndham where respect and friendship cross every boundary, and diversity is celebrated as our strength.

Our Values

  • • Unity in diversity
  • • Hospitality & dialogue
  • • Inclusion & safety
  • • Service & environmental care
Our Story

Rooted in local friendship

WIN began as a small group of neighbours and faith leaders meeting over tea to visit each other’s sacred spaces and understand one another. The welcome spread to schools, libraries and parks — and the network grew.

Today, we host interfaith visits, equality forums, wellbeing sessions and community dinners supported by partners across the West.

  1. 2012 — Beginnings

    First informal dialogues and shared meals in Werribee.

  2. 2017 — Multifaith Dinner

    Hosted our first Multifaith Dinner with community partners.

  3. 2020 — Wellbeing Online

    Mental health & music meetups kept people connected.

  4. Now — Growing together

    Regular visits, equality forums and community celebrations.

What we offer

Programs & Services

Interfaith Visits & Dialogue

Guided visits to local temples, gurdwaras, churches and more — with Q&A and shared meals.

Wellbeing & Mental Health

Tea, music and practical tools delivered with our trusted practitioners.

Equality & Respect Forums

Panels and training that promote safety, respect and bystander action.

Environment & Tree Day

Hands-on planting days and local eco-care with families and partners.

Cultural Celebrations

From Karwa Chauth to community dinners—events that showcase the joy of diversity.

Schools & Libraries Engagement

Talks and activities that build understanding with young people.
